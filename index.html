About me

So, my life's passion has always been rather,,, extraordinary pets. At the moment I have two frogs, two gecko's, nine tarantulas, a bunch of bugs, a snake and a cat. From a young age I've always done art. Usually drawing cartoons and such. In the last few years, I've been focusing on digital art and character design. My art style has been developed trough the media I've consumed in my childhood, thus i like to draw stuff that can be a bit creepy at times. Whenever I'm having a rough time getting up in the mornings, I think to myself 'build a better future for your reptiles' and then life gets a bit easier!

Experience
